❌ Car not moving?
   → Check L298N power (need 6-12V)
   → Check common GND
   → Verify pin connections
   → Run motor test section

❌ Can't connect to WiFi?
   → Look for RC-Car-ESP8266 in WiFi list
   → Wait 10 seconds after power on
   → Check serial monitor output

❌ Controls laggy?
   → Move phone closer to car
   → Reduce other WiFi interference
   → Check battery level

❌ Motors go wrong direction?
   → Swap IN1↔IN2 or IN3↔IN4
   → Or swap motor wire connections
